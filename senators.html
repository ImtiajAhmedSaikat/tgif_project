Senators

First convened in 1789,the composition and powers of the senate are established in Article one of the U.S Constitution.Each state is represented by two senators,regardless of population,who served staggered six-years term.The senate has several exclusive power not granted to the House including concentingto or confirming appoinments of Cabinet secretaries,federal judges,other federal executives officials,military officers,regulatory officials,ambassadors,and other federal uniformed officiers, as well as trial of federal officials,impeached by the House.
